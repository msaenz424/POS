I wrote this program as a hobby.

The processes it includes:

Users' login

Searches a product by typing the Product's name

Searches a product by selecting a specific Category

Add/Remove/Edit an item in the shopping cart

Registers a sale

Maintenance of Categories/Products/Employees

Shows top products sold from date1 to date2

Shows top products sold the same day

Option to Switch Languages between English and Spanish



The Database's script makes use of:

Stored Procedures,

A Trigger to update the stock of Products right after a sale is registered,

Views for Reports
